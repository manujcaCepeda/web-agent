## SERVICES TEMPLATE (DIGITAL AGENCY / WEB DESIGN) — PREMIUM VERSION

---

## CORE USER PSYCHOLOGY (CRITICAL)

The real decision-maker is:

👉 A business owner or entrepreneur who feels:

* overwhelmed by digital competition
* frustrated by past bad experiences with agencies
* uncertain about ROI
* pressured to go digital but unsure how

They are thinking:

* "Will this actually bring me more clients?"
* "Is it worth the investment?"
* "Can I trust this agency?"
* "How long will it take?"

ALL content MUST:

* reduce fear of wasted investment
* build credibility fast
* show concrete results (not just pretty design)
* communicate speed and simplicity

---

## EMOTIONAL COPY SYSTEM (MANDATORY)

Each section MUST include at least ONE emotional trigger:

Use variations of:

* "Tu negocio merece una presencia digital que venda"
* "Más clientes desde el primer día"
* "Sin complicaciones, sin demoras"
* "Diseño que convierte visitantes en clientes"

---

## HERO STRATEGY (CRITICAL)

Hero MUST communicate in 3 seconds:

1. What you do (diseño web premium)
2. Who it's for (negocios que quieren crecer)
3. Why it's different (rápido, moderno, enfocado en resultados)

Include:

* Benefit-driven headline (resultado, no proceso)
* Speed + quality trust signal
* Primary CTA (asesoría gratis / agenda ahora)

---

## SERVICE POSITIONING

Services MUST feel like business outcomes, not technical tasks.

Instead of:

❌ "Diseño de sitios web"

Use:

✅ "Un sitio web que trabaja por ti 24/7 atrayendo clientes"

Each service MUST:

* solve a specific business problem
* communicate a measurable outcome
* reduce fear or objection

---

## PRICING SECTION (MANDATORY — CRITICAL FOR CONVERSION)

MUST include a 3-tier pricing table:

* Show value at every price point
* Highlight the middle plan as "most popular"
* Each plan: clear features list + price + CTA button
* Add urgency or social proof near pricing ("Más de X negocios ya eligieron este plan")
* Use visual differentiation: middle plan larger/bolder/different bg

RULE: Pricing must NEVER feel expensive — it must feel like an investment with clear ROI.

---

## TRUST SYSTEM (CRITICAL)

### MANDATORY STATS BAR
| Stat | Default if missing |
|---|---|
| Proyectos entregados | "50+" |
| Días de entrega | "3–7" |
| Satisfacción de clientes | "100%" |
| Países atendidos | "5+" |

### MANDATORY BADGE GRID (4 authority signals)
1. Entrega Garantizada en 7 Días
2. Diseño 100% Personalizado
3. Optimizado para Móviles
4. Soporte Post-Entrega Incluido

### MANDATORY TESTIMONIALS
* Real names + role + company/city
* Each testimonial: specific outcome (more clients, more sales, better image)
* Avatar photos — NOT initials
* Stars above every quote

---

## HOW IT WORKS (MANDATORY)

3-step process:

1. Agendar asesoría gratuita
2. Diseñamos tu sitio a medida
3. Lanzamos y empiezas a recibir clientes

Closing line: "Todo el proceso toma entre 3 y 7 días. Sin complicaciones."

---

## CTA SYSTEM (HIGH CONVERSION)

Each CTA MUST include:

* low friction: "Asesoría gratuita — sin compromiso"
* speed signal: "Respuesta en menos de 24 horas"
* urgency: "Plazas limitadas este mes"

---

## LANGUAGE STYLE

Tone MUST be:

* direct and confident
* benefit-focused
* modern and energetic
* NOT technical or jargon-heavy

Avoid:

* "soluciones integrales"
* "paradigma digital"
* generic agency speak

---

## UX PRIORITY

Design for:

* fast decision making
* trust in first scroll
* clear path to contact/consultation

User should feel:

👉 "Esto es exactamente lo que necesito. Los voy a contactar ahora."

---

## FINAL RULE

If content feels:

* generic
* too technical
* without clear outcomes

→ REWRITE until it feels human, direct, and result-driven
