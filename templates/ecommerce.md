## ECOMMERCE TEMPLATE — PREMIUM VERSION

---

## CORE USER PSYCHOLOGY (CRITICAL)

The real decision-maker is:

👉 A shopper who is:

* comparing options before buying
* looking for proof the product works
* afraid of wasting money or getting a bad product
* ready to buy — but needs one final push of trust

They are thinking:

* "Is this worth the price?"
* "What if it doesn't work / doesn't fit / I need to return it?"
* "Why should I buy this here and not somewhere else?"

ALL content MUST:

* eliminate purchase hesitation
* build desire through proof (reviews, photos, results)
* reduce perceived risk (returns, guarantees, shipping)

---

## EMOTIONAL COPY SYSTEM (MANDATORY)

Each section MUST include at least ONE desire or confidence trigger:

Use variations of:

* "Loved by [X]+ happy customers"
* "Finally, a [product] that actually works"
* "The last [product] you'll ever need to buy"
* "Try it risk-free — we guarantee you'll love it"

---

## HERO STRATEGY (CRITICAL)

Hero MUST communicate in 3 seconds:

1. What the product is and who it's for
2. The primary benefit or transformation
3. Why to buy now (offer, guarantee, or urgency)

Include:

* Benefit-driven headline (outcome, not feature)
* Trust signal (reviews, customers, guarantee)
* Strong CTA with urgency or offer

Example direction:

* "Finally, [Product] That [Key Benefit] — Backed by [X]+ Reviews"
* "Shop Risk-Free — Free Returns + 30-Day Guarantee"

---

## PRODUCT POSITIONING

Products MUST be presented as solutions, not items.

Instead of:

❌ "Leather wallet with 6 card slots"

Use:

✅ "The last wallet you'll ever buy — slim, durable, and crafted to last a lifetime"

Each product MUST:

* lead with the transformation or outcome
* address the primary objection in the description
* include social proof (rating, reviews count)

---

## TRUST SYSTEM (CRITICAL)

You MUST include ALL:

* Star rating average + total reviews
* Money-back guarantee (30/60/90 days)
* Free shipping threshold or policy
* Secure checkout badges
* Real return policy summary

MANDATORY phrase:

"[X]-Day Money Back Guarantee. No questions asked."

---

## PURCHASE FRICTION REDUCTION

Include reassurance constantly:

* "Free returns within [X] days"
* "Ships in 1-2 business days"
* "Secure checkout — SSL encrypted"
* "Over [X] happy customers can't be wrong"

---

## HOW IT WORKS / ORDER PROCESS

Simple 3-step system:

1. Choose your product / size / variant
2. Secure checkout in under 2 minutes
3. Delivered to your door in [X] days

Closing line MUST include:

"Risk-free purchase. Love it or we'll make it right."

---

## IMAGE SYSTEM (STRICT)

ONLY allow images that show:

* product in use / lifestyle context
* before/after or transformation (if applicable)
* multiple angles or variants
* happy customer using the product

REJECT images if:

* product on plain white background only (add lifestyle shots)
* low resolution or pixelated
* no human element or context

Images MUST feel:

* aspirational
* high-quality
* relatable

---

## TESTIMONIAL STRATEGY

Testimonials MUST include:

* specific product benefit experienced
* before/after comparison
* clear recommendation with emotional context

Example direction:

* "I was skeptical at first, but after 2 weeks I can't imagine going back. This is the real deal."

---

## CTA SYSTEM (HIGH CONVERSION)

Each CTA MUST include:

* urgency:
  "Limited stock available" / "Sale ends [day]"

* reassurance:
  "30-day money-back guarantee"

* friction reduction:
  "Add to cart in one click — checkout in under 2 minutes"

---

## LANGUAGE STYLE

Tone MUST be:

* confident and direct
* benefit-first
* peer-to-peer (not corporate)
* specific (numbers, results, timeframes)

Avoid:

* vague superlatives ("amazing", "best ever")
* passive voice
* overly technical product specs without benefits

---

## UX PRIORITY

Design for:

* immediate product desire (hero image + headline)
* fast path to purchase (minimal steps)
* trust on every scroll (badges, reviews, guarantee)

User should feel:

👉 "This is exactly what I needed. And it's safe to buy."

---

## MICROCOPY (MANDATORY)

Use small trust phrases across the site:

* "Free shipping on orders over $[X]"
* "Rated [X]/5 by [X]+ verified buyers"
* "Ships from [location] in [X] days"
* "30-day returns — no questions asked"

---

## SECTIONS ORDER (RECOMMENDED)

1. Hero — product + benefit + CTA
2. Featured Products / Collection Highlights
3. How It Works (simple 3-step)
4. Social Proof (reviews, UGC, ratings)
5. Benefits vs. Competitors / Why Us
6. Guarantee & Shipping Policy
7. FAQ (purchase objections)
8. Final CTA / Offer
9. Footer

---

## FINAL RULE

If content feels:

* generic
* unconvincing
* missing the objection-killer

→ REWRITE until every hesitation is addressed and the next step is obvious
